SparkOut - 2026

Third-year students at Nottingham Trent University hosted their Capstone Project event to help showcase their work and support their journey into the industry. Huge thank you to Marcel Kajut for inviting me to photograph Sparkout 2026. Motorsport UK 2026

I was invited by Roman, a third-year NTU Esports Production student, to photograph their Capstone Project, Motorsport UK. At the time, I was still new to photography and videography and unsure where to start. Being invited gave me the confidence boost I needed and helped me move onto other projects, allowing me to grow and showcase my passion for photography and videography.

NSE Finals 2026

Huge thanks to Gin Rai and NTU for inviting me to photograph the behind-the-scenes production at the NSE British University Esports Championship Finals at Confetti X. This collection captures the crew, broadcast setup, and pre-show coordination—more production life than competitive play.
